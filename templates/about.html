About BugzyAI

We're on a mission to revolutionize software testing with AI-powered automation that helps teams ship better software faster.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, BugzyAI emerged from a simple observation: software testing was still too manual, time-consuming, and error-prone. Our founding team, with decades of combined experience in software development, quality assurance, and artificial intelligence, set out to solve this fundamental problem.

We believe that every software team deserves access to enterprise-grade testing tools, regardless of their size or budget. That's why we've built BugzyAI to be intuitive, powerful, and accessible to teams of all sizes.
